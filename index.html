About

A community of people who want to share, understand and reflect on each other's stories. A platform to open up conversations on everyday issues that are hushed up. A safe space to share our personal journeys, no holds barred and experience solidarity. By sharing narratives from our individually unique journeys, we can build common ground on our deepest fears and insecurities and collectively rejoice in our celebrations and growth. Further, by opening our hearts about topics considered sensitive or secretive, we also break the cycle of silent suffering and promote a culture of openness, empathy and acceptance.
